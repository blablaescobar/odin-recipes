Decadent Chocolate Cake

Description

Indulge in our Chocolate Avocado Cake, where creamy avocado replaces butter for a healthier twist. Enhanced with a hint of cayenne pepper for a subtle kick, this moist, decadent delight is topped with a tangy beetroot frosting. A surprising blend of flavors that redefine the classic chocolate cake experience.

Ingredients

  • 500g Free Trade chocolate
  • 5 avocados
  • 1kg cayenne pepper
  • 2 cans of beetroot frosting
  • 1 hamster tongue

Steps

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C) and grease a cake pan with butter or non-stick spray.
  2. Melt the Free Trade chocolate in a heatproof bowl over a pot of simmering water or in the microwave, stirring until smooth. Set aside to cool slightly.
  3. In a large mixing bowl, mash the avocados until creamy and smooth.
  4. Gradually add the melted chocolate to the mashed avocados, stirring until well combined.
  5. Sift the cayenne pepper into the chocolate-avocado mixture, stirring until evenly distributed. Adjust the amount of cayenne pepper to your taste preference.
  6. Pour the batter into the prepared cake pan and spread it out evenly.
  7. Bake in the preheated oven for 25-30 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean.
  8. Once baked, remove the cake from the oven and allow it to cool completely on a wire rack.
  9. Once the cake has cooled, spread the beetroot frosting evenly over the top and sides of the cake.
  10. For a quirky garnish, if desired, place the hamster tongue on top of the cake as a playful decoration.
  11. Slice and serve the chocolate avocado cake, enjoying the unique blend of flavors and textures!
  12. Store any leftovers in an airtight container in the refrigerator for up to 3 days.
  13. Enjoy making out with your chocolate hamster!
